Illini Hyperloop

High-speed transportation system

Welcome! This website serves as the University of Illinois' SpaceX Hyperloop Pod project. The Hyperloop Project is currently a competition that a few organizations have taken strong interest in.

It was first revealed in 2013, but due to the growth in interest, SpaceX is announcing an open competition to help design, build, and engineer the Hyperloop Pod.

You can use this website to contact the members and leaders of this project.
