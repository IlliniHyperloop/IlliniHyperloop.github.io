Kyle Weiskircher

Aerodynamics Subsystem Lead

Kyle is a Senior in Aerospace Engineering at the U of I. While growing up, his dad was in the Air Force, so ever since he was a kid he loved planes.

When he got to the University, he decided to get his license simultaneously while staying on track with the engineering curriculum. During the summers, he has done research with the Aerospace Engineering department on campus. He has always dreamed of doing something that could change the world, so as soon as he heard about the Hyperloop competition, he wanted to get involved. He sees this as an extremely exciting opportunity to show the world what Illinois is capable of, as well as a chance to offer something new and revolutionary to the world.
