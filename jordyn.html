Jordyn Robare

Team Secretary

Jordyn is a junior at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. She is from Minooka, Illinois and has lived in Illinois her whole life. She is a pre-med student, majoring in Molecular and Cellular Biology and minoring in Chemistry and Spanish.

After graduating from UIUC, she plans on pursuing her education somewhere less flat, warmer, and possibly near the ocean or mountains. She will attend medical school and complete a four-year residency to prepare for a career in anesthesiology. Jordyn is a member of the American Medical Student Association (AMSA) and the secretary for Illini Hyperloop. She became involved with Illini Hyperloop through two of her friends she met freshman year: Mizan Rahman and Seth Faletsky, the co-presidents of the RSO, and is excited to be a part of such a huge advancement in transportation technology.
