Christopher Wessel

Manufacturing Subsystem Lead

Chris is a senior in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Growing up in the northwest suburbs of Chicago, he grew up tinkering with anything mechanical. His grandfather was an avid inventor, and this inspired Chris to pursue an engineering degree.

He began his college career at William Rainey Harper College in the Chicago suburbs, where he manufactured a twelve-foot carbon fiber rocket, in his garage, for a national NASA competition through his community college. His team placed 4th in the nation, beating teams such as MIT, Georgia Tech, and U of I Urbana. This experience helped refine his manufacturing and leading abilities, as well as gain hands on experience with composite manufacturing, which will play well with his role on this team.
