Rishab Pohane

Electronics Subsystem Lead

Rishab is a first year Computer Science student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. In high school, though his life revolved around his varsity soccer team, Rishab was very involved in engineering projects both inside and outside the classroom.

From taking mechanical engineering courses to conducting grant based research projects, Rishab has dabbled in a few fields and wishes to continue expanding his horizons as a member of Illini Hyperloop. What the future will look like is of great interest to Rishab, and he does not simply want to see it happen but to be a revolutionary that helps make it happen. It is for this reason that he decided to join the Illini Hyperloop team. He hopes his various leadership experiences will help him effectively guide the Electronics Subsystem through a productive and victorious year.
