Joseph Kasson

Joe is a second semester Junior studying Mechanical Engineering. He hails from Breese, Illinois, a proud, small town of two stoplights about 40 minutes east of St. Louis. Some of his hobbies include playing sports, traveling, scuba diving, leather crafting, going to the opera and generally being adventurous.

Joe spends his time outside of class spreading the word about Illini Hyperloop, working with the Formula SAE team, and promoting campus safety through Safewalks and Student Patrol. His marketing experience stems from his role on the Student Patrol marketing team as well as his work in promoting his summer softball team and many clubs and events in high school. Joe is very passionate about the Hyperloop and its potential to change the world! He hopes to someday travel the world through Hyperloop transportation.
