Jay Mahadev

Chassis Subsystem Lead

From a young age, Janardhan had been profoundly interested in various fields of the arts and sciences. However, a grueling year designing a human colony on the surface of an asteroid for the International Space Settlement Design Competition had made the hard decision for him. He is now in his final year of mechanical engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign.

Having worked at CITL, CB&I and most recently at Littelfuse, he has been able to gain a wealth of experience solving real world mechanical, civil, computer and electrical engineering problems. His propensity for innovation, diverse technical background, and management experience, led him to take on the role as the Chassis Subsystem Lead for Illini Hyperloop, a student organization committed to designing and building the winning pod of the SpaceX Hyperloop Competition. In addition to a deep technical appreciation of the Hyperloop project, Janardhan is also confident about its socio-economic benefits as a new mode of transportation.
